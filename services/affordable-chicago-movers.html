3 Ways to Save on Your Chicago Move

We help you get the best value for your budget.

Pack Yourself

Labor is the biggest cost factor. By having everything boxed up and ready to go before we arrive, you can shave hours off the move time.

Move Mid-Week

Weekends are peak time. Scheduling your move for a Tuesday or Wednesday often ensures better availability and flexibility.

Prep Furniture

Disassembling bed frames and taking legs off tables yourself means our crew can start loading immediately, saving you billable time.

Expert Advice

Big vs. Small Moving Companies

Affordable doesn't mean taking a risk. Here's what to watch for when comparing big national movers to small, owner-operated local crews.

  • Pay for the move, not the brand: Big chains have higher overhead, and that cost often shows up in your quote.
  • Consistency matters: A smaller company can keep the same trained crew on your job instead of scrambling with random day labor.
  • Avoid the "cheap movers" trap: Some lowball quotes turn into surprise fees for stairs, wrap, or "mandatory" add-ons.
