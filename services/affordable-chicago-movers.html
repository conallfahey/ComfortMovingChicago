3 Ways to Save on Your Chicago Move

We help you get the best value for your budget.

Pack Yourself

Labor is the biggest cost factor. By having everything boxed up and ready to go before we arrive, you can shave hours off the move time.

Move Mid-Week

Weekends are peak time. Scheduling your move for a Tuesday or Wednesday often ensures better availability and flexibility.

Prep Furniture

Disassembling bed frames and taking legs off tables yourself means our crew can start loading immediately, saving you billable time.

Local Favorites

Chicago "New Move" Guide

Our team's personal recommendations for celebrating your new home.

Margie’s Candies

The Celebration

In Oak Park, we always went to Petersen's, but in the city, Margie’s Candies is the ultimate tradition. Whether you’re at the Bucktown or Ravenswood location, their world-famous hot fudge sundaes are the only proper way to toast to your new home.

Lakefront Trail

Unpack Your Mind

Boxes everywhere? Take a break. The Lakefront Trail is the best way to remember why you moved here. 18 miles of paved path with the skyline on one side and Lake Michigan on the other. It’s free, beautiful, and the perfect stress reliever.

Sawada Coffee

Morning Fuel

You need fuel for unpacking. Sawada Coffee in the West Loop isn't just strong; it's an experience. Try the Military Latte. It’s a bit of a trek if you aren't nearby, but absolutely worth the trip to kickstart your first weekend.
