Crew opportunities

Where could you fit?

We accept applications for current and future crew needs. Availability can vary with season and workload.

Hands-on role

Mover / Helper

Protect, carry, load, transport, and place customers' belongings with speed and care.

  • Safe lifting and carrying
  • Furniture and home protection
  • Positive customer communication
  • Reliable arrival and teamwork
Road and crew role

Driver / Mover

Combine careful moving work with safe, responsible operation of company vehicles.

  • Valid driver's license and safe record
  • Confident city driving and parking
  • Truck inspections and load awareness
  • Professional customer presence
Leadership role

Crew Lead

Keep the job organized, the crew coordinated, and the customer informed from start to finish.

  • Moving or field-team experience
  • Job planning and delegation
  • Calm problem-solving
  • Clear paperwork and communication
What the job requires

Be ready for real moving work.

  • Safely lift and carry heavy household and office items.
  • Work on stairs, through tight spaces, and in changing Chicago weather.
  • Follow safety, wrapping, loading, and property-protection procedures.
  • Maintain dependable transportation to assigned meeting locations.
  • Communicate respectfully with customers, teammates, and building staff.
  • Work schedules that may include early mornings, weekends, and variable end times.
