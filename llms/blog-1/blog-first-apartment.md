# First Apartment Checklist: Essentials | Comfort Moving Chicago

Moving into your first apartment is an exciting milestone! But it can also be overwhelming when you realize just how many little things you need. To help you get settled, we've put together a list of essentials for every room.

## 1. Bedroom Essentials

A good night's sleep is priority number one. Make sure you have:

- Mattress and bed frame
- Sheets, pillows, and comforter
- Hangers for the closet
- Nightstand and lamp

## 2. Bathroom Basics

Don't get caught without toilet paper on your first night! Stock up on:

- Shower curtain and liner
- Bath towels and hand towels
- Toilet paper and holder
- Trash can and plunger

## 3. Kitchen Must-Haves

Even if you don't cook often, you'll need the basics:

- Pots and pans (start with one large skillet and one sauce pot)
- Plates, bowls, and silverware
- Glasses and mugs
- Dish soap and sponge

## 4. Cleaning Supplies

Keep your new place sparkling from day one:

- Broom and dustpan or vacuum
- Multi-surface cleaner
- Paper towels and trash bags
- Laundry detergent
