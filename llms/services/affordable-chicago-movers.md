## Big vs. Small Moving Companies

Affordable doesn’t mean taking a risk. Here’s what to watch for when comparing big national movers to small, owner-operated local crews.

- Pay for the move, not the brand: Big chains have higher overhead, and that cost often shows up in your quote.
- Consistency matters: A smaller company can keep the same trained crew on your job instead of scrambling with random day labor.
- Avoid the “cheap movers” trap: Some lowball quotes turn into surprise fees for stairs, wrap, or “mandatory” add-ons.
