## Common Hoisting Questions

## What fits through a window/balcony?

We can hoist sofas, armoires, appliances, and large table tops. We'll assess the window size and clearance to ensure a safe fit before starting.

## Do you need a permit?

If we are hoisting over a public sidewalk or street, a permit might be required. For private property (like a backyard or driveway), usually no permit is needed. We can help determine this.

## Is it safe for my furniture?

Yes. We wrap every item in thick moving blankets and shrink wrap before rigging. We use specialized soft straps that don't mark or damage furniture.
