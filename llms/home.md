## Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common moving questions

### How much does a move cost?

Our [rates](https://comfortmovingchicago.com/blog/blog-understanding-moving-quotes.html) start at $150/hour for 2 movers and a truck. Final cost depends on the size of your move, distance, and any additional services like packing or furniture assembly.

### Do you provide packing materials?

Yes! We bring all necessary packing materials including boxes, tape, bubble wrap, and furniture pads. Materials are included in our packing service rates.

### How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance, especially during [peak moving season](https://comfortmovingchicago.com/blog/blog-best-time-to-move-chicago.html) (summer months). However, we often accommodate last-minute moves based on availability.

### Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely! We're fully licensed (ICC License #236923 MC) and insured. Your belongings are protected throughout the entire moving process.
