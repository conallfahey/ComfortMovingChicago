Before move day

Ravenswood moving tips before move day

A little access planning can save a lot of carrying, circling, and elevator-window stress.

  • Check whether your block has permit parking or posted restrictions.
  • Ask your building about elevator reservations, move hours, and insurance rules.
  • Reserve loading access when possible, especially near busier corridors.
  • Avoid peak commuter windows near the Ravenswood Metra area when possible.
  • Tell movers about stairs, alley access, tight turns, and long carries.
  • Photograph fragile, high-value, or oversized items before the move.
  • Schedule earlier for month-end lease turnover, when Chicago movers book quickly.
