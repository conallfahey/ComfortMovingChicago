Moving to West Loop

The West Loop is Chicago's hottest neighborhood, known for its incredible dining scene, converted industrial lofts, and booming tech corridor.

Whether you're moving into a luxury high-rise on Lake Street or a historic loft in Fulton Market, our team understands the unique challenges of city moving. We handle freight elevator reservations and COI requests daily.

Source: West Loop Community Organization

Moving Tip

High construction density; check the CDOT map for active crane permits before your move day.

Loft Logistics

We know how to navigate freight elevators and loading docks in converted warehouses and high-rises.

Tech Ready

Specialized in moving home offices for tech professionals and startups with extra care for monitors and electronics.

Neighborhood Guide

Here are a few of our favorite spots in the West Loop / Fulton Market area:

Au Cheval

Famous Eats

Famous for having one of the best burgers in America. Au Cheval is a must-visit. Expect a wait, but it's absolutely worth it for the culinary experience.

Mary Bartelme Park

Urban Oasis

A modern urban park with a unique "mist" feature. Mary Bartelme Park is a perfect spot for a dog walk, a picnic, or just enjoying the skyline view.

Soho House Chicago

Social Hub

A creative hub and luxury social club. Soho House offers a vibrant atmosphere. The Allis Bar on the ground floor is great for meetings or coffee.
