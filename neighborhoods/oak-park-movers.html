Local Guide

Settling Into the Village

Whether you’re moving from the city or across the country, Oak Park offers a blend of urban energy and suburban charm. Here is how we recommend you spend your first week in your new home:

Frank Lloyd Wright Block

Architectural Exploration

You can't live here without appreciating the genius of the Prairie School. Take a stroll down the Frank Lloyd Wright Historic District. Walking past the Home and Studio or the Unity Temple is the perfect way to ground yourself in the history of your new neighborhood. It’s world-class architecture, right in your backyard.

Buzz Café

Morning Fuel

Need a break from unpacking? Head over to the Harrison Street Arts District and visit Buzz Café. It’s been a local staple for years, offering great coffee and a vibrant, community-focused atmosphere. It’s the best place to meet your new neighbors and get a feel for the local creative scene.

Petersen’s Ice Cream

The Celebration

You survived moving day! There is no better way to celebrate than a trip to Petersen’s Ice Cream. An Oak Park institution since 1919, their old-school scoops are the ultimate reward for a job well done. We’ve celebrated many of our own company milestones here, and we think it’s the perfect "welcome home" treat for you and your family.
