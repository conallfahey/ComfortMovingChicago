Moving to The Loop

The Loop is the heartbeat of Chicago, but moving in the Central Business District requires precision. We handle the unique challenges of high-rise moving so you don't have to.

Pro Moving Tip

Confirm your building's loading dock height. Many older buildings in The Loop have clearances under 12 feet, which requires specialized trucks.

The Loop Neighborhood Guide

Discover the landmarks that make downtown living unique.

Millennium Park

Home to "The Bean" (Cloud Gate) and the Jay Pritzker Pavilion. It's your front yard for free summer concerts and winter ice skating.

The Art Institute

One of the oldest and largest art museums in the U.S. Guarded by the iconic bronze lions, it's a cultural treasure right on Michigan Avenue.

Chicago Riverwalk

A 1.25-mile long public space teeming with cafes, wineries, and architecture tours. The perfect spot for an evening stroll.
